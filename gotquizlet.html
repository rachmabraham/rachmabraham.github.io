Introduction

As part of the Google Android Basics course, our capstone project was to create a quizlet app. I envisioned a 7 question layout with a variety of answer inputs (checkboxes, radio buttons, and user text input). Depending on how many answers were answered correctly, customized messages inform the user how they did and which questions they got wrong.
